PAW-gwarts Express Training

Personalized Paw-gwarts camp

This is their Golden ticket to Paw-gwarts.

The magic that you will notice for your first visit will make you understand the reference. Paw-gwarts is a 35 day investment in your pal. They will come stay with us and receive a degree of training that only we can provide by teaching them at all hours of each day.

Paw-gwarts will teach them obedience commands, and these commands are tailored to you. If you want Elmo to mean rollover, then that is how we will teach it. Every other week we will ask to set a time to meet in house and occasionally in the store to work on training for your pal. While at Educanine, your pal will be safe and well kept. They will become kid friendly, other animal friendly, and well socialized as well as reduced anxiety. On the trainings in store, we teach them to ignore distractions and to focus on you and you only. The store could be in fire and as long as you are okay standing with them during the panic, they will be with you.

Paw-gwarts is all-encompassing and the best way to have your pal looking like Alfred.

Paw-gwarts includes:
  • 35 day training program (away from home).
  • Constant updates on status.
  • Customized training commands that are tailored to your needs and vocabulary.
  • Obedience training that includes all basic commands and some advance instructions.
  • A complete transformation in behavioral patterns.
  • Basic grooming while they attend including nail trimming, shampoo washes, and brushing.
  • Follow-up appointments to ensure proper training is sticking.
  • A complimentary treat tote, fitted collar, and extra training treats.
  • 20% off boarding a night.
  • Much more
