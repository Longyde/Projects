Who is Sabre?

Sabre is a socializing expert, fluent in German and English commands.

His skill set includes:
  • Bringing his food dish
  • Bring other items (Shoes, remote, pillows, etc)
  • Backing up
  • Recall from incredible lengths (including other rooms)
  • Scenting objects
  • Protection barking
  • Stand up on things
  • Wait
  • Spin around
  • Much more.

Sabre is a veteran Educanine helper, having assisted in dozens of dog training sessions as a mentor and a distraction. Being a German sheppard, Sabre understands the importance of emulating skills most people wouldn't imagine a dog has.

Sabre is a gentle giant who wouldn't hurt a fly, unless instructed to do so. For those new pups or old grumpy pals, Sabre is a soft tempered guy who doesn't play rough or seek a fight. He gets along with all of the above.
