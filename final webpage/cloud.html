Who is Cloud?

Cloud is our resident Diversity coach and Animal interpretation expert... Meaning he's a Cat. Cloud is the first to make an appearance to the new Bootcamp members and sometimes this is not the best choice for himself. He still has all nine lives as we put NO animal in harms way. Cloud is great for socializing puppies and aggressive dogs as he believes he is some sort of dog (or atleast plays like one).

Cloud's skill set includes:
  • Sleeping
  • Eating
  • Meowing
  • Napping
  • Purring
  • Yawning
  • Playing
  • Trickery
  • And sleeping

Cloud introduces cats and the concept of other animals in the diverse world we live in. He's been inches from husky-wolf hybrids, put bulls, bulldogs, Sabre, and all the "fierce" breeds out there. Cloud is always happy to see a new face, whether it's a nose or a snout.
